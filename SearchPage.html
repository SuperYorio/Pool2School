More Tips!

1. Remember to choose your Carpool buddies based on the common days that both of you need to go to school!
2. When you contact your potential Carpool buddy via text/phone number, remember to ask them for their schedule with his/her name on it. This is to ensure you have successfully matched with a legitimate, official Foothill student. Also, looking at each other's schedule can help set up times for Carpools!
3. If you don't find potential Carpool buddies at this moment, remember to come back and check every other week! The next registered student may live right beside you ;)
4. Keep Calm and CARPOOL!
