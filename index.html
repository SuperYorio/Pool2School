Inspiration.

As college students, we saw the need for a new form of transportation, one more flexible and convenient than public transportation, more accessible than asking friends for rides, and more affordable and environmentally-friendly than personal vehicles, Ubers, and Lyfts. Looking at community college students, we surveyed those attending school at Foothill College. Many were work/study students, for whom both the means and costs of transportation was a huge burden.

-- The Dev Team: Yorick C., Zhiwei Z., Qing H.


How It Works.

Think of Pool2School (P2S) as the "Tinder" (a famous dating app) for finding carpool buddies! P2S provides a flexible, public platform for students to search for potential carpool buddies.
